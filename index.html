Zoro Quotations
Zoro Quotations are quick and easy to create. Click the Add to Quotation button on any product page. You can then choose to create a new quotation or add the product to an existing one. Once created, quotations can easily be emailed, printed, and forwarded on. They can later be retrieved, updated, and turned into an order with just a few clicks.
The key-feature of Zoro quotations is that they will lock the very best price for that product for 14 days.
You do not need to create a Zoro account to create, manage or retrieve a quote.
Manage Quotations
Quotations are most easily retrieved through your Zoro Account, simply navigate to Quotations using the account drop down or left-hand navigation under your account . Alternatively, if you don't have a Zoro Account, quotations can be retrieved using the quotation reference on the Quotation page You can add or remove products from an existing quotation for 14 days. That's when your quote expires. You can recreate an expired quote at any time by clicking on the refresh icon in the quotation list under your account. The prices will get updated to the current website prices.
